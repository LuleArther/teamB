What is Investment Banking?

Investment banking is a specialized segment of the financial services industry that primarily focuses on providing financial advisory services and capital-raising solutions to corporations, governments, and other large institutions. Unlike commercial banks, investment banks operate in the realm of "higher finance" and complex financial transactions, facilitating capital flow between those who need funding and those with capital to deploy.
